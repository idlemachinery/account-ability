Contract Form

1. Define the task in a way that all involved could feel comfortable taking full responsibility.

Definition is required.

2. What are the Expectations?

  1. Define what a successful deliverable would look like.
  2. How is it measured? (Metrics)
  3. What format is the final product to be delivered in?
  4. Who is this ultimately for?

3. What Actions do I need to perform to complete this task or project successfully?

  1. What Authority do I need to overcome hurdles?
  2. Who do I see if I don't have Authority?
  3. What tools and resources will I need and which ones do I have?
  4. Who can I ask for help if needed?

4. What is the expected Timeline?

  1. Exact deadlines should be defined.
  2. Is this a single task or multi-phase project?
  3. Get a sense of Urgency.

5. Know the Stakes

  1. How important is this task to the project, company, or individual?
  2. What are the benefits and rewards of successfully completing?
  3. What are the consequences of coming up short or incomplete?
  4. Who is impacted (empathy)?
  5. Who decides the stakes?

6. Renegotiation (optional)

  1. What is the renegotiation process?
  2. Timing of renegotiation?
  3. Who to renegotiate with?
